# Compliance

## Compliance Objectives
- Maintain auditable onboarding records.
- Ensure lawful identity verification and consent capture.
- Enforce retention and deletion policies for sensitive data.

## Required Controls
- Immutable audit trail for stage transitions and approvals.
- Consent logs for WhatsApp agreement communications.
- Access reviews for privileged roles.
- Evidence retention policy for KYC uploads.

## Data Lifecycle
- Collect only required onboarding information.
- Archive completed applications by policy window.
- Purge expired or invalid records according to legal obligations.

## Reporting
- Compliance dashboard for pending controls and policy exceptions.
- Scheduled evidence exports for internal audits.
