# Product Epics (Execution Form)

## Epic 1: Application Intake and Eligibility
Build intake APIs/UI and CRB-powered eligibility decisions with amendment/rejection support.

## Epic 2: Device Provisioning and Payment Validation
Capture IMEI, validate downpayment evidence, and sync device enrollment providers.

## Epic 3: KYC, Client Verification, and Media Evidence
Collect and verify identity evidence, selfie, and unboxing proof with policy controls.

## Epic 4: Agreement Signing and Final Activation
Deliver agreement over WhatsApp, capture acknowledgement, and complete activation checks.

## Epic 5: Admin Operations and Role Governance
Implement role-based admin queues, approvals, overrides, and governance controls.

## Epic 6: Partner Management and Revenue Insights
Onboard partners, track performance, and expose conversion/fallout metrics.

## Epic 7: Security, Compliance, and Auditability
Enforce authentication, authorization, encryption, and auditable event trails.

## Epic 8: Observability, Runbooks, and Quality Gates
Ship production telemetry, alerting, incident runbooks, and release quality criteria.
