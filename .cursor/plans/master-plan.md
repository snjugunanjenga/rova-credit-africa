# Master Delivery Plan

## Objective
Launch a secure financing onboarding platform for partners and internal teams with complete lifecycle tracking.

## Workstreams
1. Core onboarding engine (state machine + APIs).
2. Device and payment verification integrations.
3. Admin operations and partner portal.
4. Security, compliance, and observability hardening.

## Milestones
- Foundation: schema, auth, baseline APIs.
- Workflow: eligibility -> release automation.
- Scale: partner enablement, analytics, optimization.
